The La Jolla Playhouse (14 comments)

review

Monday, July 21, 2008

Founded in 1947 by Hollywood icons, Gregory Peck, Dorothy McGuire and Mel Ferrer, the La Jolla Playhouse has a historical past. Today, The La Jolla Playhouse, offers superb live theatre performances, providing sterling entertainment. The theatre is located at the University of California, San Diego Campus.

Its impressive productions have included 42 world premieres, 7 American premiers and 24 west coast premieres. The La Jolla Playhouse has received hundreds of honors, including the coveted Tony Award for America's Outstanding Regional Theatre. read full review
